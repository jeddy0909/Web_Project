ABOUT US

So Twenty & Wendy

TwentyWendy is a Thai cosmetic brand founded in July 2020 by Love Pattranite. Driven by her love for makeup and the desire to create her own line, Love, who started the brand at the age of 20, affectionately uses the nickname "Wendy." This playful combination is reflected in the brand's name: TwentyWendy.

TwentyWendy focuses on creating high-quality, cruelty-free cosmetics that are both affordable and accessible.
